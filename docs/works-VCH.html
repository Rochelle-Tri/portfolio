A New Scheduling System

At the time, Vancouver Coastal Health (VCH) was transitioning from an outdated scheduling system to a more robust and modern platform: My Time (Infor WFM). This new system would enable employees to view their schedules, access unit schedules, submit time-off requests, and manage shift swaps or apply for additional hours. Designed to improve workforce planning and execution, My Time offered industry-specific scheduling and relief management tools built on a reliable technology platform.

My role was to design and test the first iteration of eLearning modules to train employees, schedulers, and managers on how to use the system. These modules were intended for a wide and diverse audience—serving over 30,000 front-line employees across VCH.
